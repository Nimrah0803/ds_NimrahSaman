---

## **Analysis Overview**
- Cleaned and merged datasets on `Date`.  
- Explored Closed PnL, trade size, side, and volume vs sentiment.  
- Generated visualizations for patterns and correlations.  
- Key metrics: PnL volatility, average daily PnL, trade volume by sentiment.

---

## **Key Insights**
- Greed periods → higher PnL volatility, larger trade sizes.  
- Fear periods → smaller, consistent profits, lower trade volume.  
- Trade side (Buy/Sell) behaves differently under fear vs greed.  
- Daily PnL trends correlate with market sentiment; actionable for risk-aware strategies.
